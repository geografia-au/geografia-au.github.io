Macedon Ranges Agribusiness Plan

The Macedon Ranges Agribusiness Plan recognises that agribusiness is, and will continue to be, an important part of the Shire’s economy, but that land use pressures mean the Shire needs to take a more active role in minimising conflict. At the end of the project, Geografia was employed to analyse the Shire’s business survey and an extensive survey regarding telecommunications blackspots in the region.
