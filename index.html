Our works

We take on complex multi-disciplinary projects around Australia and in South East Asia, working in economics, demography and spatial planning. A selection of the hundreds of projects we have completed are described below.

  • Economic Impact of Arts Venues in Melbourne

    There is a measurable local term impact of artist-led gentrification on Melbourne's economy

  • East Arnhem Land Economic Review

    Economic modelling and GIS outlines the potential growth opportunities in Galiwink'u, East Arnhem Land

  • Court Service Demand Modelling

    Growing demand for court services across the State

  • Penrith Economic Development & Tourism Strategy

    Land and infrastructure assets give Penrith a competitive edge

  • The Need for High Speed Broadband

    More jobs and investment if the NBN rollout prioritises business precincts over residential in Melbourne's South East

  • Loch Sport Population Study

    Can targeted public and private investment stimulate population growth in Loch Sport?

  • Business Opportunities from High Water Security in Wodonga

    Food and beverage industry prospects in Wodonga off the back of high water security

  • Georgetown Conurbation Spatial Strategy

    A strategy to improve spatial analysis across Northern Malaysia
